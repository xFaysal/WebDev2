Skis

Skis

When you are skiing, you obviously need skis. These are what separates yourself from the snow and they can range from $300-$1200!

Ski Boots

Boots

Boots are an essential part of the skiing experience. If you do not have comfortable boots, you will not have a good experience. Boots can range from $200-$800!

Bindings

Bindings

Bindings are a very small but essential piece of a ski setup. They are what keeps you and your boots attatched to the skis. There are lots of small details to look for when you are buying bindings so it gets confusing! Prices range from $100-$500

Helmet

Helmet

A Helmet is the last completely necessary part of anyone's setup. Wearing a helmet can prevent you from enduring life changing injuries. Skiing is a very dangerous sport! Prices range from $100-$400
