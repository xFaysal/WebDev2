ABOUT TYLER MILLER

He started his MLS career with the Seattle Sounders but then moved to LAFC in 2018 but recently was traded to MInnesota United FC. He won the MLS Cup in 2016 with Seattle Sounders. He played for the Under-20 US National Team and recenlty been named on the roster of the National Team.

COLLEGE AND EARLY SOCCER

He spent his whole college career at Northwestern University where he made a total of 77 appearances and ended his college career with a school record of 36 shutouts. He also played in the Premier Development League for Ocean City Nor'easters and Chicago Fire U-23.
