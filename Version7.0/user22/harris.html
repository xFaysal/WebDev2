ABOUT ASHLYN HARRIS

She was aparrt of the US team for the U-16, U-17, and U-19 teams. She played on the Undeer-21 US team but nevere got to play due to knee injuries. With the US National team she has won the 2015 and 2019 FIFA World Cup along with many CONCACAF Tournaments and SheBelieves Cup.

Currently she plays for the Orlando Pride NWSL team but has played for the NWSL since 2009. She was named NWSL Goalkeeper of the Year in 2016, she made 62 saves and had a 1.33 goals-against-average in 15 games. She had 4 clean sheets, and recorded 70 saves in the 2018 season. She also got NWSL Save of the week in the first week of the 2019 season.

COLLEGE CAREER

Over her 4 years at the University of North Carolina Tar Heel she played a total of 50 games with 15 shutouts. Her goals against average was only 0.57. She also only allowed 31 goals over her 4 year career. In the NCAA Tournament she was in the championships during 2006, 2008, and 2009.
