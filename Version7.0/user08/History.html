The Brief History Of London

The Romans founded London about 50 CE but lucky a few thousand years later The City’s Luck began to Shift in 1065, when Westminster was established. One Year Later after his victory at the Battle of Hastings, William the Bastard/Conqueror was crowned King of England, In about 1665 London was hit by the Great Plague, which killed about 100,000 people Jack the Ripper stalked the city’s women in 1888, killing at least five.
